Icons

Icons are small, simple images that enhance the effect of text or carry certain information by themselves. In most cases, these are vector images (svg). They are integrated using the <img> tag or through CSS (background and others).

Logo - content, vector, svg

Content booster, vector, svg

Content image

Content images are images that are the content of the site, mostly raster (jpg/png). jpg/png images are converted to webp. They are integrated using the <img> tag.

Content, raster (jpg to webp)

Design element

Design elements are vector or bitmap images (perhaps with transparency) that is part of the site design. Extensions: svg, jpg/png. Images jpg/png extensions are converted to webp. They are integrated through CSS (background and others).
